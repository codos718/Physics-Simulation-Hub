Gravitational Force

Gravity is the force that pulls objects toward a planet.

Different planets have different gravity:
  • Mercury: 3.7 m/s²
  • Venus: 8.87 m/s²
  • Earth: 9.8 m/s²
  • Mars: 3.71 m/s²
  • Jupiter: 24.79 m/s²
  • Saturn: 10.44 m/s²
  • Uranus: 8.87 m/s²
  • Neptune: 11.15 m/s²
This affects how fast objects fall.

This playground lets you explore gravity by dropping objects on different planets!
