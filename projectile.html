Projectile Motion Simulator

Projectile motion describes the two-dimensional movement of an object under gravity, like a thrown ball. This simulator shows how angle, velocity, and gravity affect the projectile's path.

Formulas Used: 
x = v × t × cos(θ)
y = v × t × sin(θ) − ½ × g × t²
  • Horizontal velocity is constant.
  • Vertical motion is affected by gravity.
  • The path is a parabola.
